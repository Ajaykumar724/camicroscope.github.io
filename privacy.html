Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will help you understand how caMicroscope uses and protects the data you provide when you visit and use camicroscope.org.

We reserve the right to change this policy at any time. Please visit this page frequently for updates.

What User Data We Collect

We may collect the following data:

  • Your IP address.
  • Your contact information and email address.
  • Other information such as interests and preferences.
  • Data profile regarding your online behavior.
  • Any annotations or form responses you provide.

Why We Collect Your Data

We collect your data for several reasons:

  • To better understand your needs.
  • To improve our services and products.
  • To contact you for surveys and market research.
  • For scientific objectives in pathology and related fields.

By using our website, you agree to allow cookies to collect data regarding your online behavior.

Links to Other Websites

Our website contains links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of those sites.

Restricting the Collection of Your Personal Data

You can restrict the use and collection of your personal data by leaving boxes unchecked on forms or contacting us if you've already agreed to share information.

  • The right to erasure of your personal data.
  • The right to restrict processing of your data.
  • The right to object to processing.
  • The right to data portability.
